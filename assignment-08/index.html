How to Make Slime

Ingredients
  • 8-ounce bottle Elmer's white school glue
  • 1 1/2-2 Tablespoons contact saline solution
  • 1 Tablespoon baking soda
  • Food coloring, optional

Directions

  1. Squeeze the bottle of glue into a bowl. Add your food coloring if desired and stir until combined.
  2. Mix in saline solution. If it's too sticky add more saline solution. The more you add, the thicker it'll be. The less you add, the slimier it'll be.
  3. Using your hands, knead the slime until it holds together.
  4. Store slime in a container with lid
