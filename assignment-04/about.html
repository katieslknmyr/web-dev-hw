About the Author

Edgar Allan Poe was an American writer and poet who was best known for his short stories and poems that captured imagination. His storytelling about mysteries and horror was a beginning to modern detective stories. Poe was born on January 19, 1809 in Boston, Massachusetts. He did not grow up knowing his parents very well and grew up apart from his siblings. Poe got married at the age of 13 in 1836. He died on October 7, 1849 of congestion of the brain, but the actual cause of his death has been subject of endless speculation. [Edgar Allan Poe](./Edgar-Allan-Poe.jpg) [index.html](./index.html)
